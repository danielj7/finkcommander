Updating Table Data

On startup and sometimes after a command is processed, a progress indicator will appear on the right-hand side of the status bar, and the message "Updating table data . . ." will appear on the left. This means that FinkCommander is running apt-get and fink commands to obtain the information about available packages displayed in the table. The process is similar to the one Fink itself engages in when you see the message "Reading package info . . ." in the terminal.
You can force FinkCommander to do a complete update of the table at any time by selecting File:Update Table Data.
